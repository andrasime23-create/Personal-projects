#Cloye - the ultimate tool for choosing your oufits 

Many people struggle with choosing their outfits. At Polyhack v.17 edition, me and my team developed an embedded system for choosing the outfits based on the personal wardrobe. 

The system uses a Flusk server to connect the wardrobe with the ESP32 server, for it to be shown on an LCD, based on weather and temperature + personal prefferences. It is designed as an intelligent mirror, practical for busy people that want to be in touch with fashion.
